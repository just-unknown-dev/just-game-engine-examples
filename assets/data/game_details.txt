ABOUT
-----
Just Game Engine Demo is a top-down survival arena built on the just_game_engine
ECS (Entity-Component-System) framework. Fight waves of enemies, collect coins,
and survive as long as possible. The game showcases rendering, audio, signals,
database persistence, and data-driven configuration — all in Flutter.


HOW TO PLAY
-----------
• Use the virtual D-pad (bottom-left) to move your player around the arena.
• Dodge enemies — they will seek you out and damage you on contact.
• Collect glowing coins scattered across the world to earn score.
• Survive each 30-second wave to earn a wave-clear bonus.
• When your health hits zero you lose a life. Lose all lives → Game Over.


CONTROLS
--------
  D-pad Up / Down / Left / Right   Move player
  Pause button (top-left)          Pause / Resume game
  ? button (top-right)             Show this screen


PLAYER
------
  Max Health      : 100 HP
  Lives           : 3
  Move Speed      : 220 px/s
  Collect Radius  : 35 px  (coins within this range are auto-collected)
  Invulnerability : 1.5 s  (grace period after being hit)


ENEMIES
-------
Enemies spawn at random edges of the world and move directly toward you.
They grow faster with every wave — watch out!

  Contact Damage    : 10 HP per hit
  Base Speed        : 60 px/s
  Speed per Wave    : +15 px/s
  Initial Max Count : 6 on screen at once
  Hard Cap          : 12 enemies maximum
  Contact Radius    : 30 px

Spawn interval starts at 4 seconds and decreases by 0.1s each wave
(minimum 1.5 seconds).


COINS
-----
Coins are scattered randomly across the 1 600 × 1 200 world.
Walk near one to collect it automatically.

  Target Coins on Map   : 12
  Replenish Threshold   : 3  (new coins spawn when fewer than 3 remain)
  Score per Coin        : 50 pts


WAVES
-----
A new wave starts every 30 seconds. With each wave:
  • Enemies get faster (+15 px/s)
  • Maximum enemy count increases (+1, capped at 12)
  • Spawn interval decreases (faster enemy arrivals)
  • You earn a Wave Clear Bonus of 500 pts


SCORING
-------
  Collect a coin      +50 pts
  Defeat an enemy    +100 pts
  Clear a wave       +500 pts  (wave clear bonus)

Your final score is saved to the leaderboard at game over.


WORLD
-----
  World Size      : 1 600 × 1 200 px
  Camera          : Smooth follow (lerp speed 0.08)
  Background      : Deep space (#0d0d1a)
  Boundaries      : Player is clamped — you cannot leave the arena


TIPS & TRICKS
-------------
  ★  Stay near the centre — it gives you the most escape routes.
  ★  Collect coins quickly; a full map slows enemy navigation.
  ★  After taking a hit you have 1.5 s invulnerability — use it to reposition.
  ★  Wave speed stacks every round — plan ahead for later waves.
  ★  Check the leaderboard to compare scores with past sessions.


TECH STACK
----------
  just_game_engine   ECS world, sprites, animations, camera, particles, audio
  just_signals       Reactive game state (score, health, wave, phase)
  just_database      SQLite leaderboard with aggregate stats
  just_storage       Persistent settings (name, volume, FPS toggle)
